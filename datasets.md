# Datasets

| Dataset | Description | Source | Access |
|---|---|---|---|
| GREF (Gesture Recognition with EMG and FMG) | Multimodal forearm dataset from 13 subjects containing 4-channel sEMG and 4-channel FMG signals covering 66 hand-gesture classes. Used for training and benchmarking gesture recognition models. | [TU Darmstadt TUdatalib](https://tudatalib.ulb.tu-darmstadt.de/items/dc50601f-41d8-4e4c-966b-83bf7b7f8543) | Public |
